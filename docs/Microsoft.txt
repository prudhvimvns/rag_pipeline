Microsoft Corporation is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Redmond, Washington.

Microsoft was founded in 1975 by Bill Gates and Paul Allen.

The company develops software, cloud computing services, artificial intelligence solutions, and consumer electronics.

Microsoft's major products include Windows operating system, Microsoft 365, Azure Cloud Platform, GitHub, LinkedIn, and Xbox.

Azure is Microsoft's cloud computing platform that provides computing, storage, networking, and AI services.

Microsoft has invested heavily in artificial intelligence and has partnered with OpenAI to integrate AI capabilities into its products.

The company serves businesses, governments, educational institutions, and individual consumers worldwide.

Satya Nadella is the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Microsoft.

Microsoft's headquarters is located in Redmond, Washington, United States.
